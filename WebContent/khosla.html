    Khosla Hostel


    Highlights

    1. Khosla hostel is named after the great Indian engineer and politician, Ajudhiya Nath Khosla. He was the Chairman of the Central Waterways Irrigation of India.
    2. The warden of Khosla hostel is Prof. Neelendra Badal. He is one of the prestigious faculty of the Computer Science Department of KNIT Sultanpur.
    3. Khosla hostel is meant for the allotement of third year students in addition to the Aryabhatt hostel. It is a three-seater hostel.
    4. The hostel has three floors - the ground floor,the first floor and the second floor.
    5. The hostel has a capacity to accomodate almost 200 students.
    6. It has its own mess facility. It has a gym meant for physical and recreational purposes.
    7. The space outside the hostel is used for playing sports like bollyball, kabaddi and cricket.
