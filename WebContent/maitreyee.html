    Maitreyi Hostel


    Highlights

    1. Maitreyee hostel is named after the Indian Hindu philosopher of the later Vedic period, Maitreyee. She explored the concept of 'Atman' in a dialogue.
    2. The warden of Maitreyee hostel is Prof. Varun Kumar and Prof. Harsh Vikram Singh.
    3. Maitreyee hostel is meant for the allotement of girl students of all the four years of B.Tech and including other streams.
    4. The hostel is divided in three wings - two single seater wings and one three seater wing.
    5. The hostel has a capacity to accomodate almost 280 students, the highest among all the hostels.
    6. It has its own mess facility. It has a basketball court and two courtyards.
    7. It is the biggest hostel of KNIT in terms of space and accomodation.
